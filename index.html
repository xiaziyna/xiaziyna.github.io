I am a PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I am jointly advised by Professor Farzad Kamalabadi (ECE) and Professor Athol Kemball (Astronomy) since 2017.

Before joining Illinois, I completed an Msci. in Astrophysics at Royal Holloway University of London.

My research interests are focused on signal processing applied to exoplanet science. In particular, I combine statistical inference and computational methods for transiting exoplanet telescopes. I apply my research at scale using Bluewaters.
